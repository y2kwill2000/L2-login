INJECT [STRING]

Injects a packet into the stream to the SERVER.

The packet must adhere to the following conditions to have a chance at working.

1. Use 00 instead of 0 for zero valued hex.
2. Spaces between hex pairs are allowed. (00 00 00 or 000000 will work)
3. Do NOT include the two byte size at the start of the stream. This will be calculated and appended for you.
