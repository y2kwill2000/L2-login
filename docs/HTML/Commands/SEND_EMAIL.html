SEND_EMAIL [STRING] [STRING] [STRING] [STRING] [STRING] [INT]

[STRING1] is the source email address.

[STRING2] is the destination email address.

[STRING3] is the subject of the email.

[STRING4] is the body of the email.

[STRING5] is the smtp server

[INT6] is the port to use

Send an email using the server/port specified from the account specified to the account specified.

You need some smtp server for this command to work.

EX: SEND_EMAIL "god@heaven.com" "mpj@noobmail.com" "I see what you did there..." "if you keep doing that... hair is going to grow on your palms" "smtp.heaven.com" "25"
